Privacy Policy

Last updated May 6, 2020

The short story is that we never share your data, and we minimize what we know about you. The information we do collect may be needed for a moment, but we permanently delete it as soon as it's no longer needed.

The use of FundingBox Meetings requires neither subscription and creation of an account nor registration. If you connect with FundingBox Meetings through the FundingBox Platform, we do not record this. We collect your data only in the scope necessary to provide the Service.

We do not use cookies or any other tracking technology. The list of your recent Meetings is stored with a browser technology called "local storage" which, unlike cookies, collectively never reaches our servers.

We do not use any third-party services that might put cookies in your browser. We consider the necessity of every piece of data we keep, only retaining what we need to identify and troubleshoot problems and to improve the Service. Collected data will never be used for any other purpose.

What personal information we process?

We aim to protect privacy on our platform by collecting as little user data as possible, and encouraging our users to use protective technologies as VPNs or privacy-first browsers.

We consider the necessity of every piece of data we keep, only retaining what we need to identify and troubleshoot problems and to improve the service. Collected data will never be used for any other purpose.

To provide the Service, we process network usage information, including IP addresses of the Meeting Participants and the URL used to host the Meeting.

Our servers temporarily store this information, only our system administrators and developers can access it as needed, and it is permanently deleted after seven (7) days.

During a meeting, we also collect Meeting related content, which may contain personal information. For example:

  • if you use the chat function, chat messages are stored during the Meeting.
  • in addition, users have the option to remain anonymous or provide a name and email address that will be displayed to Participants in the Meeting.

All this information is temporarily stored on our servers to enable user functionality in a video Meeting and permanently deleted as soon as the last Participant leaves the Meeting. We use Amazon (AWS) infrastructure to provide the Service.

If you use the FundingBox Meetings mobile App, we may request to access your device calendar to detect which events contain the Meeting link or allow you to add the Meeting link to existing events. In this case, information from the events in your device remains in your device and will never be transmitted to our servers.

If you email or otherwise contact us, our customer support team may also retain information you'd find in an email, like first and last name and/or email address. This data is accessed exclusively by customer support and developers as needed, and helps us provide support, fix things, and generally talk directly to you.

How is personal information used?

We use this information to provide and improve the FundingBox Meetings service. In addition, we may need to use this information to:

  • meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.
  • enforce applicable Terms, including investigation of potential violations.
  • detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security, or technical issues.
  • protect against harm to the rights, property, or safety of FundingBox, our users, or the public as required or permitted by law.

By default all Meetings are open, so anyone with the link may join. You can change it in your settings.

You can also lock the Meeting with a password, so only the people with the password can join.

We use the Gmail/Google calendar plugin which allows you to attach the Meeting link to Google events directly from the google calendar app. We do not store those data.
